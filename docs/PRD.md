# PRD: Construction Development Portugal (AI-Driven)

## Overview
A platform to identify and analyze gaps in the Portuguese real estate market for new construction development. Inspired by interactive 3D/diorama visualizations, this project aims to provide developers with a data-driven "Living Diorama" of Portuguese development opportunities.

## Objectives
- Map and analyze construction trends across Portugal.
- Identify geographic and sector-specific gaps in new developments.
- Provide a visualization layer (Interactive Playwright/3D) to simulate development impact.

## Core Features (Roadmap)
1. **Data Layer (Portugal Focus):**
   - Integration of municipal urban plans (PDM).
   - Real estate market price and demand heatmaps.
   - [ACTION] Automated download and indexing of Portugal topographic and cadastral maps.
2. **AI Analysis:**
   - Identifying under-served areas for residential or commercial development.
   - Financial feasibility simulations based on local construction costs.
3. **Visualization:**
   - Interactive 3D dioramas of sites (similar to the "Golden Gate Living Diorama" concept).
   - Computer-use automation for research and site analysis.

## Project Structure
- `/docs`: PRDs, research, and legal/regulatory notes.
- `/src`: Application source code.
- `/assets`: Map data, 3D models, and static resources.
